## Trigger
"retro", "회고", "retrospective", "reflect"

## Purpose
Close the sprint with evidence, concrete improvements, and reusable knowledge.

## Process
1. Gather factual sprint metrics: planned vs completed tasks, hill result, ship verdict, tests/build/blockers, change size.
2. Summarize what went well in no more than three points.
3. Summarize what should improve, with one concrete action per item.
4. Extract code/process/anti-patterns and classify their reuse potential.
5. Record carry-over work and the next sprint's focus.
6. Feed long-lived patterns into `pattern-extract` when broader promotion is warranted.

## Output
- Sprint retro report
- Carry-over list
- Pattern/lesson summary for future bets or sprints
